A Legacy of American Muscle Cars

Ford, Chevrolet (Chevy), and Dodge have been at the forefront of the American muscle car scene for decades. These brands have created some of the most iconic high-performance vehicles, known for their power, speed, and aggressive styling. From the golden era of the 1960s and 1970s to modern high-performance models, these brands continue to dominate the muscle car segment and maintain a loyal fanbase.

Best-Selling Muscle Cars

While the Ford Mustang remains the top seller, the Dodge Challenger has seen a surge in popularity due to its retro design and high-powered variants. The Camaro, though still a favorite, has struggled in recent years with declining sales.
